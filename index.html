Stock Market Prediction Based on News Headlines

Everyday news holds a lot of value on the daily stock market updates and this projects deals with the exact thing. The data contains the date and top 25 news for that day. On this data NLP technique called reducing stopwords is applied first, then Porter Stemmer to stem the data. Upon creating the corpus of the polished dataset, we apply logistic regression, random forest regression, Multinomial Naive Bayes and find their respective accuracy predicting the ups & downs of stock market.

Pneumonia Classification Using Neural Networks

The Project deals with a dataset consiting of more than 5000 images taken from the chest CT scans pertaining to Pneumonia patients and some are not, so we deal with first-rate classification problem. We use Convulational neural network with 5 layers and train the model, after 5 Epoch and 163 steps we achive an accucracy of 90.7% accuracy. Final results of correct and incorrect portrayal is done in a form of confusion matrix. This makes it easy to identify the quality of predicitons made.
